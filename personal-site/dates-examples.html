The datetime attribute is used to translate the date intoa machine-readbale format

Values can be represented as:

  • Full date: 2025-11-01
  • Date and time: 2025-11-01T14:30
  • Time only: 14:30
  • A Duration
  • A calendar date
  • Time on a 24-hour clock
